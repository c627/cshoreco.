Journal Entry ✨

Saved by Grace 🕊️

CshoreCo. has a deeply personal meaning that is the absolute heartbeat of everything I do.

✨ C = Christ + Cheska
🌊 shore = near the beach side
🤍 Co. = Covenant

The story of how this covenant began starts in a place I never expected. God met me in the absolute deepest, lowest, and darkest season of my life. It was a season where I felt completely isolated, where there was no one else with me. Yet, in that exact, shattering breaking moment... that is where His mighty presence flashed before my eyes. 🕊️

It wasn't intimidating; it was like a shining light, so alluring to look at and so safe to be near. He truly carries this gentle, warm, slow-glowing love that I just can't fully explain. Honestly, I would need an infinite amount of paper to describe how much His love truly, really has me in awe—a love that delights, dwells, nurtures, and above all, transforms me. ✨

Before this, I was deeply hungry for worth and attention from the people around me. Pero nung hindi na umakma sa kagustuhan ko ang mga atensyon na binibigay nila sa akin... dun na nagsimula lahat ng trahedya.

Sunod-sunod na yung financial shortage na pinagdaanan ko. Nalulong ako sa utang, na hindi ko namalayan ay malalang illegal online loans pa pala. Pero thank God pa rin, dahil iniligtas Niya ako sa kasalanang ginawa ko habang desperado akong naghahanap ng pagmamahal sa labas at sa ibang tao—mga taong hindi naman kayang ibalik ang pagmamahal na ibinibigay ko. Nandiyan din yung pagko-compromise ko sa pamilya; ibinigay ko ang mga kagustuhan nila kahit na mali na pala, hanggang sa ang lahat ng mayroon ako ay tuluyan nang nawala sa akin. 🥀

Habang naghahanap ako ng mga sagot sa sarili ko at sa ibang tao, hindi ko namamalayan na unti-unti na rin pala itong pinagmumulan ng sakit, galit, at lungkot. Dumating pa sa point na gusto ko ng isang slow, soft, bittersweet rebellion laban sa mga taong nang-betray sa akin, at sa mga experiences na sumira sa puso ko—lalong-lalo na yung pinaka-worst and most painful heartbreak na na-experience ko.

Yes, it was the most devastating heartbreak, ngunit hindi ko namalayan na ito rin pala ang magiging tanging susi sa panimula ng pagbabago ng buhay ko. Ito ang nagbukas ng pinto sa isang buhay na punong-puno ng bagong pag-asa. Grabe. Sobrang nakakataba pa rin ng puso balikan ang karanasan na ito dahil nakita ko ang lawak ng pagmamahal na tanging si Lord lang ang naging sagot sa lahat. I remember holding onto so much heaviness for the past two years of being far from His presence, living within my own understanding.

Ngayon, mas naiintindihan ko na kung bakit ganito kaganda ang salvation breakthrough testimony na mayroon ako. Yes, nawala man sa akin ang lahat—o kahit sabihin natin na inalis ni Lord yung mga tao at bagay na nagbibigay saya sa akin noon dahil hindi pala sila tunay at matatag. Sabi ko sa sarili ko, okay lang kahit kunin na ng Lord ang lahat dahil naniniwala ako na para rin naman ito sa aking kaligtasan. Pero mas higit pa pala ang plano Niya... grabe! In that exact breaking down moment ko, doon Siya nagpakita sa akin. Doon Niya ginawa ang wonderful, amazing, awesome entrance bilang isang Savior sa buhay ko, at nating lahat! That grand, beautiful entrance He did left me in total AWE of HIM! Everything happens for a reason, and truly, all of it was for His glory! ✨

And then came that overwhelming yet profoundly satisfying feeling of finally being seen exactly as you are. He was the only One who could fill that void in my heart. Siya lamang, Siya lamang ang bukod tanging nakapagbigay ng mga sagot sa bawat isa sa mga tanong na iyon na matagal ko nang hinahanapan ng kasagutan. Siya lamang ang tanging nakasagot ng tama at sakto duon sa mga tanong na hindi ko makuhaan ng sagot dito sa mundo. He was the One who fulfills everything in me—all of my hopes, all of my wonders. 🤍

Even now, I get teary-eyed just typing and sharing this. It is the beautiful, living proof of His overflowing love that truly never runs dry. 🤍

Because of this, CshoreCo. represents an eternal calling to live out my purpose here—to be a vessel and share that everlasting hope that everyone can truly have in their lives: to accept Jesus and kindly open their whole heart to Him.

This business is the fruit of labor from that wonderful salvation. It led to a beautiful breakthrough, reflecting on how I live this journey now—having the freedom to breathe, and being incredibly grateful, thankful, and blessed every single day. 🌊
